lion the king of jangel

Lion (scientific name: Panthera leo) is a species of carnivorous mammal from the panther family and the cat family. With a large body that sometimes reaches 250 kg among males, this powerful animal is the second largest member of its genus after the tiger. Because of his big body, awesomeness and agility during hunting, the lion has earned the title of "King of the Jungle"; Although it mostly lives in the hot steppes of Africa, only a few are found in the Gir forest of Gujarat, India. They feed on a wide variety of prey, including most of the ungulates native to Africa and rarely large animals such as elephants and giraffes.
